FLETCHMARKET Docs

FLETCHMARKET is an independent analytics terminal for crypto markets on Robinhood Chain — tracking onchain prices, premium gaps, lending yields, and whale flows in real time.

Network

ChainRobinhood Chain (Mainnet) Chain ID4663 Gas tokenETH Public RPCrpc.mainnet.chain.robinhood.com Explorerrobinhoodchain.blockscout.com StackArbitrum L2 on Ethereum

Data sources

Block number and gas price are read live from the public Robinhood Chain RPC via standard JSON-RPC (eth_blockNumber, eth_gasPrice). All market prices, 24h changes, market caps, volumes, and 7-day charts stream live from the CoinGecko public API (45s refresh), and the token list under "Live tokens on chain" refreshes every 30 seconds from the Blockscout explorer. The Stocks tab lists every tokenized real-world equity and ETF issued on the chain, with live price and market cap from Blockscout (60s refresh). Vault APY and TVL come from the DefiLlama yields API (5min refresh), and Chain activity streams real token transfers from the Blockscout explorer (20s refresh). If your network can't reach a given source, that panel will say so instead of showing stale numbers.

Live market data

Every number in the Markets tab is live: prices, 24h change, market cap, volume, and the 7-day charts all stream from the CoinGecko public API and refresh every 45 seconds. Alerts fire when any tracked coin moves more than ±5% in 24 hours. The Premium Gap engine (onchain AMM price vs CEX spot) will activate once Uniswap pools on Robinhood Chain are indexed.

Connect wallet

The Connect button uses your browser wallet (e.g. MetaMask) and offers to add Robinhood Chain automatically with the official network parameters above. FLETCHMARKET never asks for your seed phrase and cannot move your funds.
