How it works

Nock. Draw. Loose.

01 — NOCK

Connect a wallet

Any EVM wallet works — Robinhood Chain is fully EVM-compatible. Read-only mode needs no connection at all.

02 — DRAW

Pull the data

Pick a coin to load its real 7-day chart, or paste a wallet address to pull its balances and history straight off the chain.

03 — LOOSE

Act on the signal

Alerts fire when any tracked coin moves more than ±5% in a day, and the chain token list refreshes itself every 30 seconds.
